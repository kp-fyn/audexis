A new update is here! Audexis v0.9.5 brings OGG and OPUS support, along with various bug fixes and improvements.


## OGG and OPUS Support

Audexis now understands **OGG and OPUS metadata**.

- Reads and writes Vorbis Comments
- Handles cover images correctly (multiple images supported as well)
- Detects OGG and OPUS files more reliably
- Works alongside with other formats well


---

## Better Error Handling
Various improvements have been made to error handling when reading and writing metadata:
- More descriptive error messages
- Improved stability when encountering corrupted files
- Popups to inform users of issues during batch operations
---

##  Small Quality Improvements

- General cleanup under the hood  
- Minor UI polish  
- Stability improvements while writing metadata


If you notice anything odd, report it on [GitHub.](https://github.com/kp-fyn/audexis)
