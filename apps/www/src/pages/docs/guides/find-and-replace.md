# Find and Replace

Quickly locate and update tag values across your files.

## Open the bar

- Find: Cmd+F
- Replace: Cmd+Shift+F

The bar appears in the topâ€‘right and focuses the Find input.

## Options

- Field: choose a specific column/tag or All (find only)
- Aa: case sensitive
- Word: whole word
- Regex: regular expression

Use Enter to jump to the next match (Shift+Enter for previous). In Replace mode:

- Cmd+Enter replaces the current match
- Cmd+Shift+Enter replaces all matches
